3. **Track Sum**: Keep track of the sum of the \(B_i\) values in the heap. For each \(i\) from \(K\) to \(N\), the maximum \(A_i\) in the subset \(S\) is \(A_i\), and the sum of \(B_i\) is the sum of the smallest \(K-1\) \(B_i\) values up to \(i-1\) plus \(B_i\).
