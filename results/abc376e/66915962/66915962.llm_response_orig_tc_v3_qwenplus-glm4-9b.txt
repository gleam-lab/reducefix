2. Sorting the combined sequence \( (A_i, B_i) \) and splitting it back into \( A \) and \( B \).
3. Initializing the answer with the product of the maximum \( A_i \) and sum of the first \( K \) \( B_i \).
4. Iteratively updating the answer by replacing the largest \( B_i \) in the subset with the next \( B_i \) and updating the maximum \( A_i \).

However, there are issues with the code that lead to incorrect results, particularly for larger inputs.
