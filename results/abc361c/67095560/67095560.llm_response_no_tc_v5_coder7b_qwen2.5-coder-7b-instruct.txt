To solve this problem efficiently, we need to minimize the difference between the maximum and minimum values of the resulting sequence after removing `K` elements. The key observation is that removing `K` elements will result in a sequence with `N-K` elements. To minimize the difference, we should ideally keep the smallest possible values together and the largest possible values together.
