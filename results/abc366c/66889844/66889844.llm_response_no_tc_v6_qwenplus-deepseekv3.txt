This leads to incorrect counts of different integers when queried.

2. **Tracking Unique Integers**: Maintain a variable `unique_count` that is incremented when a new integer is added (count goes from 0 to 1) and decremented when an integer's count drops to 0 (count goes from 1 to 0).
3. **Queries**:
