The problem is in how the vertex weights are being added to the edge weights. This means that when we are choosing the next vertex to explore, we are including the weight of the outgoing vertex without subtracting the weight of the vertex itself.

The weight of vertex `u` is only added when we finally output the result for that vertex.

This correction ensures that we are only adding the weight of the edges on the path, not the weight of the vertices, to find the correct shortest path.
