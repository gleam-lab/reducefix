1. Parse the input to get the dimensions of the island (`H` and `W`) and the initial elevations of each section.
2. Initialize a priority queue to keep track of sections that are just below the current sea level.
3. For each sea level rise, update the number of sections that are still above the water by simulating the sinking of sections based on their elevation relative to the current sea level.
4. Print the area of the island that remains above water after each sea level rise.

4. **Output**: After processing each sea level, we print the remaining area of the island.
