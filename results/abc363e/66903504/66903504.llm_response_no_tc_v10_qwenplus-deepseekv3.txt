2. **Boundary Processing**:
   - Sections on the boundary of the island are the first candidates to sink as the sea level rises. These are added to the queue corresponding to their elevation.

3. **Flood Fill Simulation**:
   - For each year from 1 to Y, process all sections that will sink at the current sea level.
