Sections of the island that are adjacent to the sea or become submerged due to rising sea levels will sink. The task is to calculate the area of the island that remains above the sea level each year for the given number of years \( Y \).

### Issues in the Incorrect Code:
1. The code does not account for the dynamic nature of the sinking process where the sinking of one section can trigger the sinking of adjacent sections.
2. The code does not check for the condition that a section only sinks if its elevation is not greater than the sea level.
3. The priority queue is used incorrectly and the logic to determine the next section to sink seems off.

3. **Calculate Remaining Area**: In each year, the area that remains above sea level is the total area minus the sum of the `sinkable` sections.
