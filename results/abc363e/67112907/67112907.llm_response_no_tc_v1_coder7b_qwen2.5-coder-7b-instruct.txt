### Explanation of Changes:
1. **Initialization of Visited Array**: The `visited` array is initialized inside the `bfs` function to ensure it's reset for each sea level.
2. **Boundary Condition Handling**: The boundary condition handling ensures that only cells above the current sea level are considered as starting points for BFS.
3. **BFS Loop**: The BFS loop iterates through all neighboring cells and marks them as visited if they are within bounds, not yet visited, and their elevation is less than or equal to the current sea level.
4. **Counting Remaining Land Area**: After the BFS completes, the remaining land area is counted by checking unvisited cells.
5. **Efficiency**: The use of BFS ensures that all reachable land from the boundary is processed efficiently, and the algorithm handles large inputs within the constraints.
