2. For each sea level rise, process all sections that are currently below or equal to the current sea level.
3. Update the status of these sections and enqueue their neighboring sections if they become submerged.
4. Continue until the specified number of sea levels have been simulated.

This approach ensures that we efficiently handle large inputs and meet the performance requirements.
