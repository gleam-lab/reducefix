        int r = upper_bound(x + 1, x + n + 1, R) - x;
        // Calculate the sum of the prefix sums from l+1 to r-1

            result -= sum[x[l-1]];

The difference between the prefix sums at the upper and lower bounds gives the required result. The index adjustments for `l` and `r` in the calculation of the sum ensure that we are subtracting out the irrelevant parts of the prefix sums correctly.
