### Explanation:
1. **Sorting**: The array `A` is sorted to facilitate binary search operations.
2. **Binary Search for Each Query**:

   - Otherwise, we perform a binary search on the possible distances `d` to find the smallest `d` such that there are at least `k` points within distance `d` from `b`.
