#define vi vector<int>

1. **Reading Input**: The input values of `n`, `q`, `A` (points A), `B` (points B), and `K` (indices) are read.

2. **Sorting Points A**: The list of points `A` is sorted in ascending order.

3. **Processing Each Query**:

   - We use binary search (`lower_bound`) to find the position where `B` would fit in the sorted list of `A`.
   - Depending on whether `B` fits exactly at the found position or not, we calculate the distances to the nearest two points in `A`.
   - We output the minimum of these two distances.
