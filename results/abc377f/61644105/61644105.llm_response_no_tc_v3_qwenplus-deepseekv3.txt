3. **Inclusion-Exclusion Principle**: The code calculates the total unsafe squares by adding the squares controlled by rows, columns, diagonals, and anti-diagonals. It then subtracts overlaps (intersections) between these regions to avoid double-counting.

5. **Output**: The safe squares are computed by subtracting the unsafe squares from the total grid squares.
