1. The distance tracking is incorrect because it doesn't properly handle cycles that return to node 0.
2. The visited check is too aggressive - it prevents exploring alternative paths that might lead to shorter cycles.
3. The condition `else if(to==0)` is insufficient for detecting cycles back to node 0.
