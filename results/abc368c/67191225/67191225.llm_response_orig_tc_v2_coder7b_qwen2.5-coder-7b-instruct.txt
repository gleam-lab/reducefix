This program calculates the minimum number of attacks required to defeat all enemies. The logic behind this calculation is that each enemy requires one attack per unit of its health. Since we can only perform an attack once every three turns, we divide the total health by three to get the number of full sets of three attacks needed. If there is any remainder, we add one to account for the remaining attacks. This gives us the total number of attacks required.
