### Approaches
1. **Simulate Each Attack**: The correct approach involves simulating each attack step-by-step. For each step:
   - Increment T by 1.
   - Determine the attack strength: 3 if T is a multiple of 3, otherwise 1.
   - Attack the frontmost enemy with HP greater than 0.
   - If the enemy's HP drops to zero or below, move to the next enemy.

3. **Pattern Recognition**: For each enemy, the attacks follow a cycle where every 3 attacks (1, 1, 3) deal a total of 5 damage over 3 steps. We can compute how many full cycles (5 damage each) are needed and then handle the remaining HP.

4. **Adjust for Remaining HP**: After full cycles, the remaining HP (0 to 4) can be dealt with by considering the position in the attack cycle (T mod 3) and the remaining HP.
