### Analysis of the Problem

The game involves a sequence of attacks on enemies arranged in a line. Each enemy has a health value. The player makes repeated attacks, and the number of attacks increases by one after each enemy is defeated. The number of damage points dealt in each attack depends on whether the total number of attacks made so far (`T`) is a multiple of 3.

If `T` is a multiple of 3, each attack deals 3 damage points; otherwise, each attack deals 1 damage point. The task is to determine the smallest integer `T` for which all enemies have been defeated.
