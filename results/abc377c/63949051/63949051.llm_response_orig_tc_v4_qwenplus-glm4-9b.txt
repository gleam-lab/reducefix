2. **capture pattern analysis**: Each piece can capture pieces in an L-shape pattern. We need to determine the bounding box for each piece's capture area. This box is determined by the piece's position and the maximum L-shape distance it can capture.
3. **Conflict Detection**: For each piece, determine the bounding box of its capture area and mark overlapping boxes. This overlapping box is the area where no new pieces can be placed without being captured.
4. **Safe Squares Calculation**: The number of safe squares is the total number of squares minus the area covered by all overlapping boxes.
