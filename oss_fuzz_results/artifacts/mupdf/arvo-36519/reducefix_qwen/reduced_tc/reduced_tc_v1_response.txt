After analyzing the crash report and code, I've identified a heap-use-after-free issue in `fz_draw_clip_image_mask`. The problem occurs when `pixmap` is used after being potentially freed in the scaling block. The `fz_var(pixmap)` macro indicates this variable may be modified in the try block and needs to be preserved for the always block, but there's a risk of using a dangling pointer.

The bug is in the conditional block that scales the pixmap: if `scaled` is created, `pixmap` is dropped but then still used later in `fz_paint_image`. We need to ensure we don't use `pixmap` after it's been freed.
