After analyzing the crash and code, I can identify the root cause:

The crash is a heap-use-after-free READ 8 in `fz_draw_clip_image_mask`. The issue occurs because `pixmap` is being accessed after it might have been freed. Specifically, in the interpolation block, if `scaled` is created, `pixmap` is dropped and reassigned to `scaled`, but then `pixmap->w` and `pixmap->h` are still used in the condition check.

The bug is in the order of operations - we're using `pixmap->w` and `pixmap->h` in the condition after potentially freeing the pixmap. We need to cache these values before the potential free operation.
